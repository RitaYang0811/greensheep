品牌故事與精神

綠羊GreenSheep handmade jewelry的起源，源自創辦人那份對美的無窮追求。她追逐著優雅和美，並以自己的方式重新定義風格與流行。她深信，每一個人都應該能夠享受到獨特的珠寶飾品，而這些飾品應該是原創且擁有自信的。因此，綠羊Green Sheep誕生了，成為那個能夠實現這個信念的品牌。

The genesis of Green Sheep traces back to the founder's unwavering pursuit of beauty. With an unyielding passion, she embraced elegance and redefined style and trends on her own terms. Firmly believing that every individual deserves to enjoy unique jewelry, Green Sheep came into being—a brand that embodies originality and self-assurance.

自信 CONFIDENCE
獨特 UNIQUE
優雅 ELEGANT

綠羊GreenSheep的品牌精神是獨特而優雅的。
他代表著對自然的敬畏，彷彿迷人的綠洲，帶給人們內心的平靜與柔和。每一位佩戴者都像一隻羊，柔軟而堅定，他們不害怕與眾不同，因為他們知道自己是最優雅的綠羊。

The brand spirit of Green Sheep resonates with uniqueness and elegance. It symbolizes a reverence for nature, akin to an enchanting oasis that evokes tranquility and softness within people's hearts. Each wearer embodies the qualities of a sheep—firm and yet tender. They fearlessly embrace their individuality, knowing they are the epitome of elegance, the Green Sheep.

EMBRACE YOUR INNER GREEN SHEEP

在綠羊GreenSheep的世界裡，每一件珠寶都是一個故事的開端。它們不僅是獨特設計的結晶，更是佩戴者心靈的寄託。這些珠寶融合了璀璨的寶石與精心挑選的材料，並以台灣在地手工製作，呈現出最高品質與精湛工藝。

In the world of Green Sheep, each piece of jewelry is the genesis of a story. They are not mere products of exceptional design, but a testament to the wearer's soul. These jewels combine dazzling gemstones, carefully selected materials, and the finest craftsmanship born from local artisans in Taiwan.

綠羊GreenSheep的珠寶不同於市面上的批發品，他們是經過設計師的巧思與創意結合而成，專為那些追求個人風格、堅持原創的人而設。綠羊GreenSheep希望透過這些珠寶，讓每一位佩戴者感受到自身的獨特與美麗，而他們就是 這個世界上最獨一無二的存在。

Green Sheep jewelry sets itself apart from wholesale accessories found elsewhere. The designs are a fusion of the designer's imagination and creativity, catering to those who seek personal style and appreciate the essence of originality.
